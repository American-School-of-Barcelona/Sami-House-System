Bulk Import Students

Quickly add multiple students at once using CSV upload or homeroom-based entry.

üìÑ Option 1: CSV Upload

Upload a CSV file with student information. Perfect for importing entire grade levels at the start of the year.

CSV Format Required:
  • first_name - Student's first name
  • last_name - Student's last name
  • email - Student's email address
  • house - House name (Athena, Poseidon, Artemis, or Apollo)
  • class_year - Class year (Freshman, Sophomore, Junior, or Senior)

Example CSV:

first_name,last_name,email,house,class_year
John,Smith,john.smith@asbarcelona.com,Athena,Freshman
Emily,Johnson,emily.johnson@asbarcelona.com,Poseidon,Freshman
Michael,Williams,michael.williams@asbarcelona.com,Artemis,Freshman

üè† Option 2: Homeroom Bulk Add

Quickly add multiple students from the same homeroom/house. Perfect for transfer students or quick additions.

How it works:
  1. Select the house and class year for all students
  2. Enter student names (one per line or comma-separated)
  3. Email addresses are automatically generated as firstname.lastname@asbarcelona.com
üí° Tip: Enter "FirstName LastName" on each line, or use "FirstName, LastName" format

üí° Tips for Bulk Import

  • CSV Method: Best for importing 50+ students at once, especially at the start of the year
  • Homeroom Method: Best for smaller groups or when all students are in the same house
  • Duplicate Protection: The system will show errors for duplicate email addresses
  • House Names: Must match exactly: Athena, Poseidon, Artemis, or Apollo (case-insensitive)
  • Class Years: Must be: Freshman, Sophomore, Junior, or Senior (case-insensitive)
